## Guiding Principle

The current site sells Daniel's time. The revamped site sells AI-delivered outcomes. Every page should answer: "What does this company do for me?" not "What does this person know?"

The visual identity (DEW+Co branding, `//SECTION_NAME` coding aesthetic, clean dark/teal palette) is strong and should be preserved. The content needs to be gutted and rebuilt.

## Page-by-Page Specification

### 1. HOME (/)

**Remove:**
- "From AI Experiments to AI Results" tagline
- "I help companies stop piloting and start shipping" hero copy
- "I build AI solutions that work, then train your team to own them"
- The three-pillar model (Process Mapping → Implementation → Team Enablement)
- "I take on 2-3 clients at a time" scarcity language
- "Fractional CTO & AI Advisory" as headline positioning

**Replace with:**

**Hero Section:**
- New tagline: "Workforce Intelligence, Delivered" (or similar — test variations)
- Subhead: Position DE Williams as the company that tells organizations which roles AI will reshape, when it will happen, and what to do about it
- Primary CTA: "Take the Free Assessment" → links to /assessment
- Secondary CTA: "See Our Services" → links to /services

**Problem Section (//THE_PROBLEM):**
- Rewrite from "AI purgatory" to the real problem: organizations don't know which roles are at risk, which are safe, and which will transform. They're either panicking or ignoring it. Both responses are expensive.
- Reference the Anthropic Economic Index finding: 94% theoretical capability vs. 33% observed usage. The gap is closing, and most companies don't have a plan.

**What We Do Section (//WHAT_WE_DO):**
- Three-part model rewritten for outcomes, not process:
  1. **Assess** — AI-powered workforce resilience scoring. Every role analyzed against automation capability, observed adoption, and industry-specific factors.
  2. **Advise** — Strategic playbooks for workforce transition. Not generic "upskill everyone" advice; role-specific, timeline-aware action plans.
  3. **Transform** — Organizational readiness programs. Restructuring, reskilling roadmaps, and ongoing monitoring as AI capabilities evolve.

**Proof Section (//PROOF):**
- Keep the dollar-figure case studies ($2.5M TaxAct, $28K→$4M, $5M NFL, etc.) but add a new row for workforce intelligence proof points as they develop
- Add: "Powered by the WARE Framework" with a brief description and link to /lab
- Add partner logos when available (IncioX, Tandemly)
- Potential funders/customers to reference when appropriate: JFF, JFFLabs, Britebound, Gitlab Foundation

**How It Works (//HOW_IT_WORKS):**
- Rewrite the three-step process to reflect the tiered model:
  1. **Start Free** — Take the individual assessment. Get your AI resilience score in 5 minutes.
  2. **Go Deeper** — Upgrade to Pro for a detailed role analysis with action plan ($49-79).
  3. **Scale Up** — Organizational assessments, executive advisory, and enterprise programs for teams and companies.

**CTA Section (//WORK_WITH_ME):**
- Replace "I take on 2-3 clients" with something that signals scale: "From individual assessments to enterprise transformation, we meet you where you are."
- Primary CTA: "Start Your Free Assessment"
- Secondary CTA: "Talk to Our Team"

### 7. ASSESSMENT (/assessment) — NEW PAGE

**This is the most important new page. It's the top of the GTM funnel. Must ship tonight as a waitlist.**

#### Phase A: Waitlist (ships tonight)

- Headline: "How Resilient Is Your Role?" (or similar)
- Subhead: "We're building a free AI resilience assessment powered by the WARE Framework. Be the first to get your score."
- Brief value prop: what the assessment will tell you (resilience score, task-level risk analysis, personalized action plan)
- Waitlist signup form:
  - Email (required; this is the lead capture)
  - Job title (required; feeds future segmentation)
  - Industry (dropdown; required)
  - Years of experience (optional)
- Submit → confirmation message: "You're on the list. We'll email you when the assessment goes live."
- GA4 event: fire on successful form submission (event name: `waitlist_signup`)
- Social proof if available: "Join X others on the waitlist" (counter optional at launch)

**Data storage:** HubSpot (same CRM already used for the /contact page). Leads go directly into the CRM pipeline, enabling automated email sequences when the live assessment launches.

**HubSpot form implementation:**
1. Create a new HubSpot form with fields: email, job_title (text), industry (dropdown), years_experience (number, optional)
2. Two integration approaches (Claude Code agent should confirm which matches the existing /contact implementation):
   - **Option A: HubSpot embedded form** — Use the `@hubspot/react-form` package or the HubSpot form embed script. Simplest approach; HubSpot handles validation, submission, and CORS.
   - **Option B: HubSpot Forms API** — POST to `https://api.hsforms.com/submissions/v3/integration/submit/{portalId}/{formGuid}` from a custom React form. More control over styling to match the DEW+Co aesthetic.
3. Option B is recommended since the site has a strong visual identity that a HubSpot embed may not match.

**HubSpot follow-up automation:**
- Tag waitlist signups with a list/property (e.g., `waitlist_assessment = true`)
- When WARE goes live, trigger an automated email: "Your assessment is ready"
- Segment by industry for future targeted outreach

**Implementation notes for Claude Code agent:**
- Stack: React on S3 + CloudFront (static site)
- HubSpot is already integrated on /contact — match that pattern
- The /assessment route needs to be added to the React router
- GA4 is already in place; fire a `waitlist_signup` event on successful submission
- Match the existing site's visual identity (DEW+Co branding, //SECTION_NAME aesthetic, dark/teal palette)
- HubSpot portal ID and form GUID should be stored as environment variables or config constants

#### Phase B: Live Assessment (next week, when WARE is ready)

- Same page, swap the waitlist form for the live assessment intake
- Assessment intake form:
  - Job title (text input)
  - Industry (dropdown)
  - Years of experience (optional)

- Submit → results page with:
  - Resilience score (0-100)

  - Top 3 tasks in your role most exposed to automation
  - Top 3 tasks that are automation-resistant
  - Upgrade CTA: "Want the full analysis? Unlock your Pro report for $49-79"
- Email the waitlist when this goes live
