# dewilliams.co Revision Plan, WARE-Informed

Generated March 25, 2026 | Based on WARE v2 Assessment (72/100)

Standard title going forward: **Fractional CTO & AI Transformation Officer**

---

## STRATEGIC DIRECTION

The homepage currently positions D.E. Williams & Company as a workforce intelligence product company (WARE assessment SaaS funnel). The WARE v2 assessment says this is the most vulnerable framing:

- Automation Capital at 42/100: the product positioning lacks defensibility
- Deployment Control at 88: embedded advisory leadership is the structural moat
- Relationship Lock-In at 72: named client relationships with switching costs are the differentiator

**The revision flips the hierarchy:** lead with embedded advisory, use WARE and the newsletter as assets that power the practice, offer the free assessment as an entry point rather than the entire value proposition.

**Current:**
```

```

**Revised:**
```
Most companies are stuck between AI ambition and AI execution.

They have pilots running, vendor demos stacking up, and a leadership team
asking "what's our AI strategy?" But nothing is in production. Nothing is
making money. The gap between "this is promising" and "this runs our
business" requires more than tools. It requires someone who has closed
```

**Why:** The current version frames the problem as a data gap (which WARE solves as a product). The revised version frames it as an execution gap (which embedded advisory solves). This matches the WARE v2 finding that the advisory work is most resilient.
